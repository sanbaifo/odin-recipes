No-Bake Peanut Butter Banana Protein Bars

Description

A nutritious, simple and delicious gluten free snack.

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas
  • ¾ cup freshly ground peanut butter
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • ¾ cup oat flour
  • ½ cup coconut flour
  • ¼ cup pea protein powder

Directions

  1. Puree bananas, peanut butter, and honey in a food processor until smooth. Place mixture in a bowl and add oat flour, coconut flour, and pea powder. Stir until combined.
  2. Press into a 10-inch square pan and refrigerate until hardened, about 30 minutes.
  3. Enjoy!
